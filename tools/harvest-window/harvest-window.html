How the Harvest Window Tool Works

Upload a close-up photo of your cannabis trichomes and our AI model instantly analyzes the ratio of clear, cloudy, and amber trichomes to determine your optimal harvest window.

Understanding Trichome Colors

  • Clear trichomes — Too early. THC hasn't fully developed.
  • Cloudy/milky trichomes — Peak THC. Energetic, cerebral high.
  • Amber trichomes — THC converting to CBN. Sedative, body effect.

Most experienced growers harvest at 70-90% cloudy with 10-30% amber for balanced potency and effect.
